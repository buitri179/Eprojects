Welcome to Our Aquarium!

The National Aquarium is a nonprofit organization that connects people with nature to inspire compassion and care for our ocean planet. This wonderful place consists of various species of fish in the world.

Our Mission

To help visitors have a big view of fish,also to preserve some of them which are facing to the extinction

Popular Questions

Q: How many species of fish do you have in the aquarium?
A: We currently have over 200 different species of fish, ranging from colorful tropical fish to exotic marine species.
Q: Can I bring my own pet to the aquarium?
A: Unfortunately, we do not allow visitors to bring their own pet into the aquarium for various reasons, including the health and safety of our aquatic residents.
Q: How much money to entry?
A:4$ for children who below 18 years old and 6$ for adult
