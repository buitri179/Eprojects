Welcome to National Aquarium

Our Mission

The National Aquarium is dedicated to inspiring conservation of the world's aquatic treasures. We strive to create awareness about the importance of marine ecosystems and to foster compassion for aquatic life.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals who are passionate about marine conservation and education. We have over 20 employees who are committed to providing exceptional experiences for our visitors.

Popular Questions

Q: How many species of fish do you have in the aquarium?

A: We currently house over 200 different species of fish, ranging from colorful tropical fish to exotic marine species.

Q: Can I bring my own pet to the aquarium?

A: Unfortunately, we do not allow visitors to bring their own pets into the aquarium for various reasons, including the health and safety of our aquatic residents.

Q: How much is the entry fee?

A: Admission fees are $6 for adults and $4 for children under 18 years old.
