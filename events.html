Big Fish, Little Fish

Schedule: 18h-19h30 April 14

*Special Animal: Otters*

Did you know that sea otters have the densest fur of any animal? During this 1½ hour program, you and your child will:

– Touch tidepool animals in our private classroom touch pool

– Take a guided tour of the Northern Pacific Gallery

– Create a fun, ocean-themed craft to take home

– Forage for food like a sea otter

– Touch animal biofacts relating to sea otters
