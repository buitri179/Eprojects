Gallery

Clown Fish
  • Found in warm Pacific and Indian Ocean waters.
  • Symbiotic relationship with sea anemones.
Jelly Fish
  • Gelatinous creatures with stinging tentacles.
  • Found worldwide in oceans.
Killer Whale
  • Largest dolphin family members.
  • Apex predators found globally.
Seal
  • Semi-aquatic mammals in coastal waters worldwide.
  • Streamlined bodies with flippers.
Star Fish
  • Marine invertebrates with radial symmetry.
  • Found worldwide, known for regenerating lost limbs.
Shark
  • Apex predators with cartilaginous skeletons.
  • Found worldwide, vital for marine ecosystems.
Lobster
  • Crustaceans with hard exoskeletons.
  • Found worldwide on ocean floors.
Stingray
  • Flat-bodied fish with venomous barbs.
  • Found in tropical coastal waters.
Penguin
  • Flightless birds adapted to aquatic life.
  • Found in the Southern Hemisphere.
Puffer Fish
  • Inflate their bodies for defense.
  • Found in tropical and subtropical waters.
Sail Fish
  • Fast-swimming fish with sail-like dorsal fins.
  • Found in warm ocean waters
Salmon
  • Anadromous fish migrating between rivers and oceans.
  • Found primarily in the Northern Hemisphere.
