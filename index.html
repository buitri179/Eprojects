Nexus Aquarium - Best place for learning and family activities

One of the most popular aquarium in Washington. We have a wide range of sea creatures, about 100+ species are living in our aquarium. We also organize school events for the purpose of study. Most of the children love marine life animals.

About The Marine Life Animals

There are lots of species in our aquarium like different kind of fish, starfish, penguin, jellyfish and more. Children love coming here to learn about the animals and we have collaborated with many primary, secondary schools.

Upcoming Events

Big Fish, Little Fish

Big Fish Little Fish classes invite adults and children 3-6 years old to learn together through play, movement, and exploration.

Ocean Investigators

Do you know a child who loves the ocean? Ocean Investigators is a series of programs at the Aquarium for participants 7-12 years old, that encourages exploration of marine life.

Aquarium on Wheels

Our Aquarium educators come to YOU, bringing California marine inhabitants such as sharks, sea stars, and anemones in this dynamic mobile tidepool exhibit. Great for festivals, fairs, and school events!
